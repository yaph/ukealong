About UkeAlong

UkeAlong is a website for ukulele players featuring play along songs with information on chords and chord overview pages with fretboard charts to help with learning how to play chords in different inversions across the fretboard of the ukulele.

The play along videos are published by several YouTube channels listed on the Resources page and linked to below the videos. If you dig these videos please support their creators by subscribing to their channels, liking the videos and sharing them with your friends. Also check the respective video descriptions on YouTube to see how you can support these creators financially, if you have the means to do so, to enable them to educate and entertain people through music.

UkeAlong was created and is maintained by Ramiro Gómez. I'm an independent author and programmer from Berlin, who loves music and playing the ukulele. I created this site to be able to navigate these ukulele play along videos via chords and song keys, to avoid the distraction on YouTube and focus on the music. If you find this website useful please let your ukulele playing friends know about it.
