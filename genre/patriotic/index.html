  • The Mormon Tabernacle Choir - Anchors Aweigh

    April 26, 2019

    For educational use. The US Navy Song. It's just the chorus, and it's been digitally altered to lower the pitch 3 semitones so beginner ukulele player can learn the song. There are chords, the melody line, lyrics, and ukulele tab. I recommend using https://youloop.io/ to slow it down and isolate phrases / measures to work on.
