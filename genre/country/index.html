  • Dixie Chicks - Wide Open Spaces

    April 17, 2019

    Chords: F, B-flat, C, Gm (similar to F...just moved over and with the 3rd finger) Strum Patterns (per chord picture)?...D-D-D-DU (quick shuffle rhythm) or D- -- D- DU ....you can do the first pattern during the rockin' refrain and the second pattern during the thoughtful/peaceful verses.
