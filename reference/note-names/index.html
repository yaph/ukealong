Note Names

This reference table shows the names of the 7 natural notes and the 5 flats and sharps in English, French, German, Italian and Spanish. English uses the letters A to G, hence musical alphabet, German A to H and the 3 Roman languages use do re mi fa sol la si. A handy chart to quickly look note names in different languages.
