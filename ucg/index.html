Two ways to quiz yourself

Most players can strum along when a chord name floats overhead. Fewer can look at a chord diagram and instantly know what to play. Ukulele Chord Quiz closes that gap. Two quiz modes test you in both directions: see the shape, name it; see the name, find the shape. The chord pool is drawn from analysis of over 3,800 ukulele play-along videos, so you spend your time on the shapes that actually come up.
