Muffin Songs - Are You Sleeping

For educational use. Optimized for GCEA Ukulele. Key of C.

As performed by Muffin Songs (https://youtu.be/Ai9A_yvzPbQ). Original is in the Key of D, pitch has been dropped to the Key of C.

This play along uses the string colors of the KIDS Ukulele Strings, as made by Aquila. Individual sets are priced to make a donation of a set of strings to organizations like Ukulele Kids Club; Classroom sets (20 pack) can be purchased at a VERY affordable price directly from Aquila.

The colored font is a part of Chordette for Education, available at ukefarm.com

Genres: Folk
