The Spaniels - Goodnight Sweetheart

For educational use. Optimized for GCEA ukulele. Key of G.

As performed by The Spaniels.

They played this at a baseball game I was at last night (St. Paul Saints). A nice slow progression of chords in G (and some nice “color” chords as well.

Video created on the iPad Pro using Luma Fusion. The ukulele chord font is Chordette for Education, available at ukefarm.com.
