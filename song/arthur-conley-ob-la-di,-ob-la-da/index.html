Arthur Conley - Ob-La-Di, Ob-La-Da (Single)

This version is the familiar Beatles tune but performed by Arther Conley which is a key that's better for ukulele Key of G Chords used: G, C, D7, Bm7, Em Strum pattern: DU XU DU XU (X= chucking/chunking/palm-muting) OR you can leave out the "X" and strum DU --U DU --U
