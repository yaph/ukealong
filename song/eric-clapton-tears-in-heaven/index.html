Eric Clapton - Tears in Heaven

For educational use.

Chords Used: F, C, Dm Bb, A, Gm, Cm, D, C7

Tears in Heaven by Eric Clapton. This version does not have the bridge. It’s been lowered by 4 semitones to the key of C so beginner ukulele students can play along. Some of the more complex chords have been simplified or skipped.
