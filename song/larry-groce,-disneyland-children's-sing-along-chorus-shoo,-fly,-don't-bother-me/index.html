Larry Groce, Disneyland Children's Sing-Along Chorus - Shoo, Fly, Don't Bother Me (Vocal)

For educational use. Optimized for GCEA ukulele. Key of G. As performed by Larry Groce and the Disneyland Children's Sing-Along Chorus. Original video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Tlk6fjZ0nf4

The original version of this song is in G, and the audio has been lowered a whole step to be in the Key of F (this Key was a special request). Look for the version in G as well, as that is also a good key for ukulele..

Video created on the iPad Pro using Luma Fusion. The ukulele chord font is Chordette for Education, available at ukefarm.com

Genres: Folk
