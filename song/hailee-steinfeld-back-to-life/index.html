Hailee Steinfeld - Back to Life (from "Bumblebee")

This tutorial will show you how to play Back To Life on the ukulele by Hailee Steinfeld. The chords used are G A Bm Em F#m. (half step down from original key)

*Note - if you are adding keys/piano I would have them play a Gmaj7 instead of the G.
