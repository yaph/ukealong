Sounds Of Christmas - Do They Know Its Christmas

Ukulele play along of Do They Know It's Christmas as recorded for Band Aid in 1984. Written by Bob Geldof and Midge Ure.

Using the chords: C F G Am Dm C7

Strum Pattern: D DU U DU works for most of the song, you might want to try D DU D DU towards the end of the song.
