Dave Matthews Band - Ants Marching

Ukulele Play Along guide with chords and optional improvisation suggestion.

Chords used: D, G, Em, A7sus (a two-finger chord, very easy because it occurs right after Em and involves just modifying the Em chord)

Improv section is a modified blues scale.
