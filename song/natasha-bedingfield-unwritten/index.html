Natasha Bedingfield - Unwritten

This tutorial will show you how to play Unwritten on the ukulele by Natasha Bedingfield. The chords used are G F C Eb Em Am. (Original in F)

*Tips - Some might find it easier to play a Cm instead of the Eb. Also Em7 instead of the Em might be easier in some spots where the chord changes are faster.
