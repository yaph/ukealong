Kenny Loggins - Danger Zone

For educational use.

As performed by Kenny Loggins. The pitch has been lowered to make it playable by young ukulele students.

Chords Used: • Mostly Dm & C • There's one Gm, one F, and one A • There are technically some Bb’s in the song, but I just simplified and put Dm’s there as my students do not know Bb.
