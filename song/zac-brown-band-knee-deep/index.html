Zac Brown Band - Knee Deep (feat. Jimmy Buffett)

Knee Deep" by Zac Brown Band featuring Jimmy Buffet I transposed the song down to the key of D to make it more friendly for the ukulele.

This video features: --demonstration of the "hammer-on" technique --a hammer-on pattern to play throughout the song --a strumming pattern for chords that can be played throughout the song --you may choose to switch back and forth between playing the hammer-on pattern and chords. (The play-along screen is split so you see both the hammer pattern and the chords.)

A fun song for summer!
