Jack Johnson - My Own Two Hands

This is a ukulele play-along for the song "My Own Two Hands" as performed by Jack Johnson and Ben Harper; simply added chords to the existing YouTube video by EricaElliptical (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mRjDBd6tBBY)

The blog post that led to the creation of this play along: http://motleymuse.blogspot.com/2017/03/lower-school-uke-jam.html

Chords used: G, C, D, and E minor.

The ukulele chord font comes from an app/font set called Chordette for Education by ukefarm.com.

Genres: Folk
