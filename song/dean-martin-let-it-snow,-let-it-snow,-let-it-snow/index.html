Dean Martin - Let It Snow, Let It Snow, Let It Snow

For Educational Use. Optimized for GCEA ukulele. Key of C.

As performed by Dean Martin...however, I ended the video after the instrumental break as the ending experiences a half step key change...making the ending too hard to play for many players (C#, G#7, etc.).

Created with Luma Fusion on the iPad Pro, ukulele font is Chordette for Education, available at ukefarm.com

Genres: Holiday
