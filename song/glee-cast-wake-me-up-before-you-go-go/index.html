Glee Cast - Wake Me Up Before You Go-Go (Glee Cast Version)

For educational use. Optimized for GCEA ukulele. Key of C.

The original song is by Wham, in C. This version was originally in Bb (transposed up a whole step) as perfomed by the cast of Glee.

I had made a version of the original Wham! recording, but it was blocked in many countries. This version seems to be used for lyric videos, so hopefully it will be accessible. It is a very good cover of the original.

Video created on the iPad Pro using Luma Fusion. The ukulele chord font is Chordette for Education, available at ukefarm.com.
